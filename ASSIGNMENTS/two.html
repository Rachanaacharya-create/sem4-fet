The Rise of Artificial Intelligence in Everyday Technology

What Is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

How AI Is Used Today

AI is already part of many everyday technologies. Smartphones use AI for voice assistants, cameras, and predictive typing. Streaming platforms recommend movies .

The Future of AI

As technology continues to evolve, AI is expected to play an even bigger role in industries like healthcare, transportation, and education.
